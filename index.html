Cebu–Bohol Educational Tour & Industry Exposure
A professional learning journey focused on industry immersion, real-world IT applications, and career development.

Tour Overview

The Cebu–Bohol Educational Tour was designed to provide BSIT students with hands-on exposure to real-world information technology environments and professional industry practices. Conducted from November 12–15, 2025, the tour included visits to leading IT companies, research institutions, and public safety organizations, allowing students to connect classroom learning with practical applications.

  • Objectives: Industry immersion, practical IT exposure, career readiness
  • Focus Areas: UP IN IT UP CEBU INFORMATION CENTER, RIVAN IT, DYNATA PHILIPPINES INC, MATA TECHNOLOGY, T.A.R.S.I.E.R. 117
  • Learning Outcomes: Enhanced technical skills, professional awareness, innovation-oriented mindset
